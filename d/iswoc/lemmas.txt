æfter se hi twegen he wesan ætywan on oðer hiw hi on se tun faran
& hi þa faran & se oðer cyþan
& hi hi ne gelifan
þa æt-nyhstan he ætywan hi twelf þær hi ætgædere sittan & tælan hira ungeleaffulness & hira heorte heardness forðam þe hi ne gelifan se þe he geseon of deaþ arisan
& he secgan hi
faran into eal middangeard
& bodian godspell eal gesceaft
se þe gelifan & gefullian beon se beon hal
soþlice se þe ne gelifan se beon geniðerian
þes tacn fylgean se þe gelifan
on min naman hi deofolseocnes utdrifan
hi sprecan niwe tunge
nædre hi afyrran
& hi ne derian þeah hi hwæt deadbærlic drincan
ofer seoc hi hira hand settan
& hi beon hal
and witodlice Dryhten hælend siððan he to hi sprecan he wesan on heofon afon
& he sittan on God swiþ healf
soþlice hi þa faran æghwær bodian Dryhten midwyrcan & trymman spræc æfterfylgan tacn
on frymþ wesan word
