
 giedd wrecan ful geomorre 
 minre sylfre sið 
 yrmþa gebad aweox 
 niwes eald micel 
 hosp wite winnan wræcsiþa 
 ærest hlaford gewitan heonan leodum 
 yþa gelac habban uhtceare 
 hwær leodfruma land 
 feran gewitan folgað secan 
 wineleas wræcca minre weaþearfe 
 onginnan monnes maga hycgan 
 dyrne geþoht todælden 
 gewidost woruldrice 
 libban laðlicost longade 
 het hlaford herheard niman 
 ahte leofra lyt londstede 
 holdra freonda hycgan geomor 
 ful gemæcne monnan findan 
 heardsæligne hygegeomorne 
 mod miþendne morþor hycgendne 
 bliþe gebæro ful beotedan 
 gedælde nemnan deað 
 owiht elles onhworfen 
 
 freondscipe sculan feor neah 
 min felaleofan fæhðu dreogan 
 hatan mann wunian wudu bearwe 
 actreo eorðscræfe 
 eald eorðsele oflongad 
 denu dimme duna uphea 
 biter burgtunas brerum beweaxne 
 wic wynn leas ful wraþe begietan 
 fromsiþ frean frynd eorþe 
 leofe lifgende leger weardiað 
 uhtan gonge 
 actreo eorðscrafu 
 sittan mot sumorlangne dæg 
 wepan wræcsiþas 
 earfoþe 
 modceare minre gerestan 
 eall longaþes life begietan 
 hosp sculan geong mann geomormod 
 heard heorte geþoht sculan 
 bliþe gebæro breostceare 
 sinsorgna gedreag gelong 
 weorold wyn ful wide fah 
 feorres folclondes freond siteð 
 stanhliþe storme behrimed 
 wine werigmod wætre beflowen 
 dreorsele dreogeð wine 
 modceare gemunan 
 wynlicran wic wa sculan
