
 brucan weorþan 
 het hyssa hwa hors forlætan 
 feor afysan gangan 
 hicgan hand hige god 
 offan ærest onfindan 
 eorl yrhðo geþolian 
 let handon leofne fleogan 
 hafoc holtes hild stop 
 mann miht oncnawan cniht 
 wacian wigan wæpnum feng 
 willan eadric ealdor gelæstan 
 frean gefeohtan onginnan beran 
 gar guþe habban god geþanc 
 hand healdan miht 
 bord bradswurd beot gelæste 
 ætforan frean feohtan sculan 
 byrhtnoð onginnan beorn trymian 
 rad rædde rinc tæhte 
 sculan standan stede healdan 
 biddan hyra randas rihte heoldon 
 folman forhtedon na 
 habban folc fæger getrymmed 
 lihte leodon leofost 
 heorðwerod holdost witan 
 stod stæðe stiðlice clypian 
 wicinga ar word mælde 
 beot abead brimliþendra 
 ærænde eorle ofre stod : 
 " sendan sæmen snelle 
 heton most sendan raðe 
 beagas gebeorg god 
 garræs gafol forgyldon 
 heard hild dælon 
 þurfan spillan spedaþ 
 willað gold grið fæstnian 
 gif þat gerædest ricost 
 leoda lysan willan 
 syllan sæmannum hyra sylfra dom 
 feoh freode niman frið 
 willian sceattum scype gangan 
 flot feran friþ healdan " 
 byrhtnoð maþelian bord hafenode 
 windan wacne æsc word mælde 
 yrre anræd ageaf andswaru : 
 " gehyrst sælida folc segeð 
 willað gafol garas syllan 
 ættrynne ord eald swurd 
 heregeatu hild deah 
 brimmanna bodian abeod ongeagn 
 secgan leodum miccle laþre spell 
 stynt unforcuð eorl werod 
 wil gealgean eþel þes 
 æþelredes eard ealdor min 
 folc folde fealle 
 hæðen hild heanlic þinceð 
 ure sceattum scype gangon 
 unbefohtene feor hider 
 yrnan eard becomon 
 sceole softe sincan gegangan 
 sculan ord ecg geseman 
 grim guðplega gofol syllon " 
 het bord beran beorn gangan 
 easteðe stodon 
 miht wæter werod : 
 flowende flod ebban 
 lucan lagustreamas lang þuhte 
 hwanne togædere garas beron 
 pantan stream prasse bestandan 
 eastseaxena ord æschere 
 miht hyra derian 
 flanes flyht fyllan gename 
 flod gewitan flota stodon gearowe 
 wicinga wiges georne 
 het hæleða hleo healdan bricge 
 wiga wigheardne haten wulfstan 
 cafne cynn ceolan sunu 
 forma mann franca ofsceat 
 baldlicost bricge stop 
 stodon wulfstane wiga unforhte 
 ælfere maccus modige 
 ford fleam gewyrcan 
 fæstlice fynd werian 
 wæpna wealdan moston 
 ongeaton georne gesawon 
 bricgweardas biter findan 
 onginnan lytegian laðe gystas 
 bædon upgangan aginnan moston 
 ford faran feþan lædan 
 eorl onginnan ofermode 
 alyfan land laþere ðeode 
 onginnan ceallian cald wæter 
 byrhtelmes beirnan ( beorn gehlyston ) : 
 " gerymed ricene 
 guma guþe god witan 
 wælstow wealdan mote " 
 wodon wælwulfas ( wæter murnan ) 
 wicinga werod west pantan 
 scir wæter scyldas wegon 
 lidmen land linde bæron 
 ongeagn grama gearowe stodon 
 byrhtnoð beorn bord het 
 wyrcan wihagan werod healdan 
 feondum feohtan neh 
 tir getohte tid cuman 
 fæge mann fealle sculan 
 wearð hream ahafen hremmas windan 
 iernan æses georn eorþe cyrm 
 leton folman feolhearde speru 
 gegrundene garas fleogan 
 boga bysig bord ord onfeng 
 biter beaduræs beorn feollan 
 gehwæðere hand hyssas lagon 
 wund wearð wulfmær wælræste geceosan 
 byrhtnoðes bil wearð 
 swustersunu forheawen 
 wærð wicingum wiþerlean agyfen 
 gehyrde eadweard sloge 
 sweord swenges wyrnde 
 fot feoll fæge cempa 
 ðeoden þanc gesæde 
 burþene byre habban 
 stemnetton stiðhicgende 
 hysas hild hycgan georne 
 ord ærost miht 
 fægean mann feorh gewinnan 
 wiga wæpnum wæl feol eorþ 
 stodon stædefæste stihte byrhtnoð 
 biddan hyssa hycgan wigan 
 denon willan dom gefeohtan 
 wod wiges heard wæpen ahebban 
 bord gebeorg beorn stop 
 anræd eorl ceorle 
 æghwæðer hyra yfel hycgan 
 sendan særinc suþerne gar 
 wundian wearð wiga hlaford 
 sceaf scylde sceaft toberstan 
 spere sprengan springan ongeagn 
 gegremod wearð guðrinc gare stingan 
 wlancne wicing windan forgifan 
 frod fyrdrinc let franca wadan 
 hysses hals hand wisode 
 færsceaðan feorh geræhte 
 oðer ofstlice sceat 
 byrne toberstan breostum wund 
 hringlocan heorte stod 
 ætterne ord eorl bliþra— 
 hloh modi mann sæd metod þanc 
 dægweorces forgifan 
 forlet drenga daroð hand 
 fleogan folman gewitan 
 æþele æþelredes þicgan 
 healf stod hyse unweaxen 
 cniht gecampe full caflice 
 bræd beornan blodigne gar 
 wulfstanes beirnan wulfmær geong 
 forlet forheardne faran ongeagn 
 ord gewod eorþe licgan 
 þeoden þearle geræhte 
 gesyrwed eorle— 
 willan beorn beagas gefecgan 
 reaf hring gerenod swurd 
 byrhtnoð bræd bill sceðe 
 brad bruneccg byrnan sloh 
 raþe gelette lidmanna 
 eorles earm amyrde 
 feoll folde fealohilte swurd— 
 miht gehealdan heard mece 
 wæpnes wealdan git cweðan 
 har hilderinc hyssas byldan 
 biddan gangan god geferan— 
 miht fot leng gestandan 
 heofenum wlat : 
 " geþancie ðeoda waldend 
 wynn weorold gebad 
 ac milde metod micel þearf 
 gaste god geunnan 
 sawul siðian mote 
 geweald þeoden 
 friþ ferian— frymdi 
 helsceaðan hynan moton " 
 heowon hæðen scealcas 
 begen beorn big stodon 
 ælfnoð wulmær begen lagon 
 onemn hyra frean feorh gesellan 
 bugan beaduwe 
 wurdon oddan beirnan ærest fleam : 
 godric guþe godan forlet 
 mænigne mear gesellan 
 ( gehleop eoh ahte hlaford 
 gerædum riht ) 
 broðor begen ærndon 
 godwine godwig guþe gyman 
 wendan wigan wudu sohton 
 fleon fæsten hyra feore beorgan 
 mann micel mæð 
 gif geearnunga gemunan 
 duguþ gedon habban 
 offa dæg asæde 
 meþelstede gemot habban 
 modelice manig spræcon 
 þærfe þolian 
 wearð afeallen folc ealdor 
 æþelredes eorl gesawon 
 heorðgeneatas hyra heorra licgan 
 wendan wlance þegenas 
 unearge mann efstan georne 
 willan twega 
 lif forlætan leofne gewrecan 
 byldan beirnan ælfrices 
 wiga winter geong word mælde 
 ælfwine ( ellen sprecan ) : 
 " gemunað mæla meodo spræcon 
 bence beot ahofon 
 hæleð heall heard gewinnan : 
 cunnian cennan 
 wylle æþelo gecyþan 
 myrcon miccles cynnes— 
 ealdafæder ealhelm haten 
 wis ealdorman woruldgesælig 
 þeode þegenas ætwitan 
 fyrd feran willan 
 eard gesecan ealdor ligeð 
 forheawen hild hearma micel : 
 æghwæðer hlaford " 
 fæhðe gemunan 
 ord geræhte 
 flota folc folde licgan 
 forwegen wæpne onginnan wine manian 
 frynd gefera 
 offa gemælde æscholt asceoc : 
 " ælfwine hafian gemanode 
 þegenas þearf þeoden lið 
 eorl eorþ þurfan 
 æghwylc oðer byldan 
 wiga wigan wæpen mæge 
 healdan heard mece 
 gar god swurd godric hæfð 
 earh oddan beirnan beswicene 
 wendan formoni mann meare rad 
 wlancan wicge hlaford 
 forðam wearð felda folc totwæmed 
 scyldburh tobrocen abreoðe angin 
 manig mann aflymde " 
 leofsunu gemælde linde ahebban 
 bord gebeorg beornan oncwæð : 
 " gehate heonan nelle 
 fleon fotes trym willan furðor 
 wrecan gewinnan min winedrihten 
 þurfan ymb sturmere stedefæste hælæð 
 word ætwitan wine gecranc 
 hlafordleas ham siðie 
 wendan wigan sculan wæpen niman 
 ord iren " ful yrre wod 
 feohtan fæstlice— fleam forhogode 
 dunnere daroð acwehte 
 unorne ceorl clypian 
 biddan beorn byrhtnoð wræc : 
 " na wandian wrecan þenceð 
 frean folc feore murnan " 
 feores recan 
 onginnan hiredmen heardlice feohtan 
 gram garberend god bædon 
 moston gewrecan hyra winedrihten 
 hyra feondum fyllan gewyrcan 
 gysel onginnan geornlice fylstan 
 norðhymbron heard cynn 
 ecglafes beirnan æscferð nama 
 wandian na wigplegan 
 fysde flan genehe 
 hwilum bord sceat hwilum beornan tæsde : 
 ymb stunde sellan windan 
 wæpna wealdan moste 
 git ord stod eadweard langian 
 gearo geornful gylpword sprecan 
 fleogan fotmæl land 
 bæc bugan beterian leg 
 brecan bordweall beorn feohtan 
 sincgyfan sæmannum 
 wurðlice wrecan wæle læge

 sibyrhtes broðor manig

 wælstow wund sweltan
