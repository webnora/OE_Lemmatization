
 anhaga ar gebideð 
 metudes milts modcearig 
 lagulade lange sculan 
 hreran hondum hrimcealde sæ 
 wadan wræclastas wyrd ful arædan 
 eardstapa earfeþa gemyndig 
 wraþra wælsleahta winemæga hryre : 
 " sculan uhtna gehwylc 
 cearu cwiþan cwic nan 
 modsefan min durran 
 sweotule asecgan soþe witan 
 eorle indryhten þeaw 
 ferðlocan bindan 
 healdan hordcofan hycge willan 
 werig mod wyrd wiðstondan 
 hreo hycgan helpan gefremman 
 domgeorne dreorigne 
 hyra breostcofan bindað 
 modsefan min sculan 
 earmcearig eðle bidæled 
 freomægum feor feterum sælan 
 gear goldwine min 
 hruse heolstre biwrah hean 
 wod wintercearig waþema gebindan 
 sohte seledreorig sinc brytta 
 hwær feor neah findan magan 
 meoduhealle witan 
 freondleasne frefran willan 
 wenian wynn witan cunnað 
 sliþen sorg gefera 
 lyt hafað leofra geholena 
 warað wræclast nales windan gold 
 ferðloca freorig nalæs folde blæd 
 gemunan selesecgas sincþege 
 geoguþ goldwine 
 wenede witan wyn gedreas 
 witan sculan winedryhtnes 
 leofes larcwidum lange forþolian 
 sorg slæp somod ætgædre 
 earm anhogan gebindað 
 þinceð mode mondryhten 
 clyppan cyssan cneo lecgan 
 honda heafod 
 geardagum giefstolas breac 
 onwæcneð wineleas guma 
 gesiht biforan fealu weg 
 baþian brimfuglas brædan feþer 
 hreosan hrim snaw hagle mengan 
 hefigran heorte benne 
 sar swæsne sorg geniwad 
 mecgan gemynd mod geondhweorfeð 
 greteð gliwstafum georne geondsceawað 
 secg geseldan swimmað wegan 
 fleotendra ferð bringeð 
 cuþ cwidegiedda cearo geniwad 
 sendan sculan swiþe geneahhe 
 waþema gebindan werigne sefan 
 geþencan weorold 
 hwan modsefa gesweorce 
 eorl lif geondþence 
 færlice flet ofgeafon 
 modge maguþegnas middangeard 
 dogra dreoseð fealleþ 
 weorþan wis wer age 
 wintra dæl woruldrice wite sculan geþyldig 
 sculan hatheort hrædwyrde 
 wacan wiga wanhydig 
 forht fægen feohgifre 
 gielp georn geare cunnan 
 beornan sculan gebidan beot spriceð 
 collenferð cunnan gearu 
 hwider hreþra gehygd hweorfan willan 
 ongietan sculan gleaw hæle gæstlic 
 weorold wela weste stondeð 
 missenlice middangeard 
 windan biwaune weall stondaþ 
 hrime bihrorene hryðge ederas 
 woriað winsalo waldend licgað 
 dreame bidrorene duguþ gecrong 
 wlonc weall wig fornom 
 ferian forðwege sum fugel oþbær 
 heanne holm sum hara wulf 
 deaðe gedælde sum dreorighleor 
 eorðscræfe eorl gehydde 
 yþde eardgeard ælda scyppend 
 burhware breahtm leas 
 eald ent geweorc idlu stodon 
 wealsteal wise geþencan 
 deorc lif deope geondþenceð 
 frod ferðe feor gemunan 
 wælsleahta worn acwið : 
 " hwær cuman mearg hwær cuman mago hwær cuman maþþumgyfa 
 hwær cuman symbla gesetu hwær seledreamas 
 beorht bune byrnwiga 
 þeodnes þri þrag gewitan 
 genap nihthelm 
 stondeð laste leofre duguþ 
 wealh wundor heah wyrmlicum fah 
 eorl fornoman asca þryþe 
 wæpen wælgifru wyrd mære 
 stanhleoþu stormas cnyssað 
 hrið hreosende hruse bindeð 
 winter woma won 
 nipeð nihtscua norþan onsendeð 
 hreo hæglfare hæle anda 
 earfoðlic eorþe romana 
 onwendeð wyrd gesceaft weoruld heofon 
 feoh læne freond læne 
 mann læne læne 
 eorþe gesteal idel weorþeð " 
 snottor mode gesittan sundor rune 
 til treowe gehealdeþ sculan torn rycene 
 beornan breostum acyþan nemþe bote cunnan 
 eorl elne gefremman wel ar seceð 
 frofre fæder heofon fæstnung stondeð
