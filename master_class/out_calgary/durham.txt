
 burch breome breotenrice 
 steppa gestaðolad stanas ymbutan 
 wundor gewæxen weor ymbeornad 
 ea yðum stronge ðer inne wunað 
 feola fiscian kyn floda gemonge 
 gewexen wudafæstern 
 wuniad ðem wycum wilda deor 
 deope dæl deora ungerim 
 ðere byri bearn gecyðed 
 arfesta eadig cudberch 
 clene cyning heafud 
 osuualdes engel leo aidan bisceop 
 eadberch eadfrið æðele geferes 
 ðer inne midd æðelwold bisceop 
 breoma bocera beda boisil abbot 
 clene cudberte gecheðe 
 lerde lustum lara wel genom 
 eardiæð ðem eadige ðem minstre 
 unarimeda reliquia 
 monia wundor gewurðað writ seggeð 
 midd ðene drihnes wer domes bideð
