
 ælfred unsceððiga æþeling æþelrædes 
 sunu cinges hider inn willan modor win- 
 cestre sittan geþafian godwine eorl ec 
 mann micel mihton wealdan forðam hleoðrode 
 toward haraldes þeah unriht 
 godwine gelette hæft settan 
 gefera todraf mislice ofsloh 
 mann feoh sellan hreowlice acwellan 
 mann bende mann blendan 
 hamelode hættode 
 wearð dreorlicre dæd gedon þison eard 
 siððan denu comon frið namon 
 gelyfenne ðan leofan god 
 blission bliðe criste 
 scylde earmlice acwellan 
 æþeling lyfode git yfel mann gehet 
 mann gerædde mann lædde 
 eligbyrig gebindan 
 sona lende scype mann blendan 
 blind brohte munecon 
 þær wunian lyfode 
 siððan mann byrigde wel gebyrian 
 ful wurðlice weorþ 
 westende styple ful gehende 
 suðportice saul criste
