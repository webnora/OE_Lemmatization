
 þes singan siðgeomor findan 
 seocum sefan samnian wide 
 æðelingas ellen cyðdon 
 torhte tireadige twelfe 
 dædum domfæste dryhtne gecorene 
 leofe life lof wide springan 
 miht mærðo middangeard 
 þeodnes þegnian þri unlytel 
 halga heape hlyt wisode 
 dryhtnes æ dema sculan 
 reccan rinc romebyrig 
 frame fyrdhwate feorh ofgefon 
 þurg nerones nearo searu 
 petrus paulus apostolhad 
 wide geweorðod werþeoda 
 andreas achagia 
 egias aldre geneðde 
 þreodode þrymm ðeodcyninges 
 ænig eorþ ece geceosan 
 langsum lif sunnan unhwilen 
 siððan hildeheard here byrhtme 
 guðplegan gealgan þehte 
 gehyrdon iohanne 
 æglæawe mann æðelo reccan 
 mann gefrege 
 cneoris criste libban 
 wer hade siððan wuldor cyning 
 ordfruma eorþ sohte 
 fæmne hrif fæder manncynn 
 effessia 
 leode lærde wegan 
 siþ gesohte swegl dreamas 
 beorht boldwelan broðor læt 
 siðes sæne sweordes bitan 
 iudeum iacob sculan 
 herode ealdor gedælan 
 feorh flæsc philipus 
 asseum ece lif 
 rode cwealm ricene gesohte 
 siððan galgan gearapolim 
 ahon hildecorðre 
 huru wide wearð wurd undyrne 
 indeum aldre gelædan 
 beaducræftig beornan bartholameus 
 hatan astrias albano 
 hæðen hygeblind heafde beneotan 
 forðam hæðengild hyran willan 
 wig weorðian wuldor dream 
 lifwela leofra leas god 
 thomas þriste geneðde 
 indea dæl 
 manig wearð mod onlihted 
 hige onhyrded halig 
 siððan collenferð cyning broðor 
 awehte weorodum wundorcræfte 
 dryhtnes miht deaðe arisan 
 geong guðhwæt gad nama 
 folc feorg gesellan 
 wesan sæcce sweordræs forniman 
 hæðen hand halga gecrang 
 wund weorudum þanan wuldor sunnan 
 sawel gesohte sigor leane 
 gehyrdon þurg halige bec 
 sigelwarum soð yppe wearð 
 dryhtlic dom god dæg or onwoc 
 leohtes geleafa land gefælsod 
 matheus mære lar 
 het irtacus yrnan hycgan 
 wælreow cyning wæpnum aswebban 
 hyrdan iacob ierusalem 
 sacerdum swilt þrowian 
 ðurg stenges sweng stiðmod gecrang 
 eadig æfestum hafað ece lif 
 wuldorcining wiges leane 
 næron tohtan sæne 
 lindgelaces land persea 
 sohton siðfrome simon thaddeus 
 beorn beadorofe wearð begen samod 
 endedæg æðele sculan 
 wæpenhete weorc þrowigan 
 sigelean secan soðan gefean 
 dream deaðe gedæled wearð 
 lif lænan gestreon 
 idle æhtwelan forhogodan 
 æðelingas gesellan 
 twelf tilmodige tir unbræcne 
 wegan gewitt wuldor þegen 
 beornan lufian 
 þysses giddes begangan geomrum 
 halga heap helpan 
 friðes fultomes freonda beþearf 
 liðra lade sculan lang ham 
 eardwic uncuþ gesecan 
 lætan laste lic eorþ dæl 
 wælreaf wunigean weormum hroðre 
 findan foreþances gleaw 
 lysteð leoðgiddunga 
 fitt fegde standeþ 
 eorl eorþ brucaþ moton awa 
 woruldwunigende sculan gedreosan 
 eðle tohreosan 
 læne lices frætewa efne toglideð 
 cræft neosað 
 niht nearowe ligeð 
 cyning þeodom cunnan miht 
 word wer oncyðig 
 gemyndig mann lufian 
 galdor begangan geoc 
 frofre friclu feor heonan 
 elles eardes neosan 
 sið asettan nat hwær 
 weorold wic uncuþ 
 eard eðel ælc mann 
 nemþe godcundes gastes brucan 
 ac utu geornor god cleopigan 
 sendan usse ben beorht gesceaft 
 botles brucan motan 
 ham hehðo hihta micel 
 cyning clænum gildeð 
 lean unhwilen hosp lof standeð 
 micel mære miht seomian 
 ece edgiong gesceaft
