
 tweode 
 gifena ginnan grund þær gearu findan 
 mundbyrd mære þeodne ahte micel þearf 
 hyldo hehstan dema hehstan brogan 
 gefriðode fyrmþ waldend hyre fæder roderum 
 torhtmod tiðe gefremian ahte trumne geleafa 
 hosp eallmihtig gefrægen holofernus 
 winhatan wyrcean georne wundor þrymlic 
 girwan swæsendo het guma baldor 
 yldestan ðegnas ofstum micel 
 ræfndon rondwiggende comon rican þeodne 
 feran folc ræswan feorðan dogore 
 iudith he gleaw geðonce 
 ides ælfscinu ærest gesohte 
 simble sittan 
 wlance wingedrince weagesiðas 
 beald byrnwiggende bolle steape 
 beran bencum gelome bunan orcas 
 full fletsittendum fæge þegon 
 rofe rondwiggende ric wendan 
 egesful eorl wearð holofernus 
 goldwine guma gytesalum 
 hloh hlydde hlynede dynede 
 magan fira beirnan feorran gehyran 
 stiðmoda styrmde gylede 
 modig medugal manian geneahhe 
 bencsittende gebærdon wel 
 inwidda eal dæg 
 dryhtguman sinnan drencan wine 
 swiðmod sinc brytta swiman lagon 
 oferdrencte duguðe deaðe geslean 
 agotene god het guma aldor 
 fylgan fletsittendum fira bearn 
 nealæhte niht þystru het niða geblonden 
 eadigan mægþ ofstum fetigan 
 bedreste beagum gehlæste 
 hring gehrodene hraðe fremman 
 anbyhtscealcas ealdor bebeodan 
 byrnwigena breogo bearhtme stopon 
 gysterne iudithðe 
 findan ferhðgleawe fromlice 
 lindwiggende lædan onginnan 
 torhtan mægþ træfe hean 
 ric he restan symbel 
 niht inne nergende lað 
 holofernus eallgylden 
 fleohnet fæger folctogan 
 bed ahongen bealofulla 
 miht wlitan wiga baldor 
 æghwylcne inne 
 hæleða bearn he 
 mann cynn nymðe modiga hwa 
 niðe rofra near hete 
 rinc rune gegangan restan gebrengan 
 snude snoteran ides stercedferhðe 
 hæleð hearra cyþan halige meowle 
 gebrengan burgetelde wearð brema mode 
 bliðe burh ealdor þohte beorht ides 
 widle womme besmitan willan wuldor dema 
 geðafian þrymm hyrdan ðinges gestyrde 
 dugeða waldend gewitan deofulcunda 
 galferhð guma ðreate 
 bealofull beddes neosan sculan blæd forleosan 
 ædre binnan niht habban gebidenne 
 eorþ unswæslicne swylcne wyrcan 
 þearlmod ðeoden guma þenden ðysse weorold 
 wunian wolcen hrofe gefeallan wine drincan 
 ric restan midde nytan ræda nan 
 gewitlocan wiggend stopon 
 inne ofstum micel 
 wer winsade wærlogan 
 laðne leodhatan læddon bedd 
 nihsta siþ nergendes 
 þeowen þrymful þearle gemyndig 
 atol eaðost miht 
 ealdor benæman unsyfra 
 womfull onwoce geniman wundenlocc 
 scyppendes mægþ scearpne mece 
 scurum heard sceaðe abredan 
 swiðran folm onginnan swegl weard 
 naman nemnan nergend 
 woruldbuendra acwæð : 
 " fyrmþ god frofre 
 beirnan alwaldan biddan wylle 
 milts þearfendre 
 ðrynesse ðrym þearle wesan 
 heorte onhæted hige geomor 
 sorh gedrefan forgif swegl ealdor 
 sigor soðne geleafa þis sweord mote 
 geheawan þes morðres brytta geunnan gesynta 
 þearlmod þeoden guma nahte 
 milts micel þearf gewrecan mihtig 
 torhtmod tires brytta wesan torne mode 
 hat hreðre " hehsta dema 
 ædre elne onbryrde deð an gehwylcne 
 herbuendra he helpan seceð 
 ræde rihte geleafa wearð hyre rume mode 
 haligre hyht geniwod geniman hæðena mannan 
 feax sinum teon he folm hyre weard 
 bysmerlice bealofullan 
 list alede laðne mannan 
 unlædan eaðost miht 
 wel gewealdan sloh wundenlocc 
 feondsceaðan fagum mece 
 heteþoncolne healf forcearf 
 sweoran swiman licgan 
 drincan dolhwund dead git 
 eall orsawle sloh eornoste 
 ides ellenrof siþ 
 hæðena heafod windan 
 flore licgan fula leap 
 gesne beæftan ellor hwerfan 
 neowelne genyðerad 
 susle gesæled siððan 
 wyrmum bewindan wite gebunden 
 heard gehæfted hellebryne 
 hinsiðe ðearf hopian 
 þystru forðylmed ðonan mote 
 wyrmsele wunian sculan 
 awa aldre 
 heolstran ham hyhtwynna leas 
 habban gefeohtan foremærne blæd 
 iudith guðe hyre god uðe 
 swegl ealdor hyre sigor onleah 
 snotere mægþ snude gebrohte 
 herewæðan heafod blodig 
 fætelse hyre foregenga 
 blachleor ides hyra begea nest 
 ðeawum geðungen onlædde 
 heolfrig hyre hond ageaf 
 higeðoncolre ham beren 
 iudith gingran sinre gegnum þanonne 
 ides ba ellenþriste 
 becomon collenferhðe 
 eadhreðige mægþ herian 
 sweotollice geseon magan 
 wlitegan burh weall blican 
 bethuliam beahhrodene 
 feðelaste onettan 
 glædmode habban 
 wealgate wiggend sæton 
 wer wæccende weard heoldon 
 fæsten folc 
 geomormodum iudith bebeodan 
 searoðoncol mægþ sið gewitan 
 ides ellenrof cuman 
 leof leodum lungre het 
 gleawhydig wif guma sum 
 ginnan burh hyre togeanes 
 ofostlice forleton 
 weall gietan acwæð 
 sigefolce : " 
 þoncwyrðe þingan þyrfen leng 
 murnan mode wesan metod bliðe 
 cyning wuldor : gecyðed wearð 
 weorold wide wesan wuldorblæd 
 torhtlic toweard tir gifeðe 
 læðða lange dreogan " 
 wurdon bliðe burhsittende 
 siððan gehyrdon halige sprecan 
 heanne weall herian lustum 
 fæstengeates folc onette 
 wer wif somod wornum heapum 
 ðreatum ðrymmum þringan yrnan 
 ongeagn þeodnes mægþ þusendmælum 
 eald geong æghwylcum wearð 
 mann medobyrig mod areted 
 siððan ongeaton iudith cuman 
 eðle ofostlice 
 eaðmedum forleton 
 gleawe het gold gefrætewod 
 hyre ðinenne þancolmode 
 herewæðan heafod onwriðan 
 behðe blodig ætywan 
 burhleodum hyre beaduwe gespeow 
 sprecan æðele folc : 
 " magan sweotol sigerofe hæleð 
 leoda ræswan laðestan 
 hæðenes heaðorinces heafod starian 
 holofernus unlyfigendes 
 mann micel morðra gefremian 
 sarra sorh swyðor git 
 ycan willan uðe god 
 lengran læððum 
 egle moste ealdor oðþrong 
 god fultum guma gehwæne 
 þyssa burgleoda biddan wylle 
 randwiggendra recen 
 fysan gefeohtan siððan fyrmþ god 
 arfæst cyning eastan sendan 
 leohtne leoman berað linde 
 bord breostum byrnhomas 
 scir helm sceaþa gemong 
 fyllan folctogan fagum sweordum 
 fæge frumgaras fynd wesan eowere 
 gedemed deaðe dom agon 
 tir tohtan getacnod hafað 
 mihtig hand " 
 wearð snelra werod snude gegearewod 
 cenra campe stopon cynerofe 
 secg gesiðas bæron sigeþufas 
 foron gefeohtan gerihte 
 hæleð helma haligan burh 
 dægred dynedan scildas 
 hlude hlummon hlanca gefeon 
 wulf walde wanna hrefn 
 wælgifre fugel wistan begen 
 þeodguman þohton tilian 
 fyllu fægum fleah last 
 iernan ætes georn urigfeðera 
 salowigpada singan hildeleoð 
 hyrnednebba stopon heaðorincas 
 beorn beadowe bord beðeahte 
 hwealf lindum 
 elðeodigra edwit þoledon 
 hæðen hosp heard wearð 
 æscplegan forgieldan 
 assyrium siððan ebreas 
 guðfanum habban 
 fyrdwicum fromlice 
 leton fleogan flana scuras 
 hildenædran hornbogan 
 strælas stedehearde styrmdon hlude 
 gram guðfrecan garas sendan 
 heard gemang hæleð yrre 
 landbuende laðum cynn 
 stopon styrnmode stercedferhðe 
 wrehton unsofte ealdgeniðlan 
 medowerige mund bregdan 
 scealcas sceaðum scirmæled swyrd 
 ecgum gecoste slogon eornoste 
 assiria oretmæcgas 
 niðhycgende nan sparedon 
 herefolces heanne ricne 
 cwicera mann ofercuman mihton 
 magoþegnas morgentid 
 ehton elðeoda 
 ongeaton gram 
 herefolces heafodweardas 
 swyrdgeswing swiðlic eowdon 
 wer ebrisce word 
 yldestan ealdorþegnum 
 cyþan wrehton cumbolwigan 
 forhtlice færspel bodedon 
 medowerigum morgencollan 
 atol ecgplegan ædre gefrignan 
 slegefæge hæleð slæp tobredon 
 bealofullan burgeteldes 
 werigferhðe hwearfum þringan 
 holofernus hogedon aninga 
 hyra hlaforde hild bodian 
 ærðon egesian sæte 
 mægen ebrea myntan 
 beorn breogo beorht mægþ 
 wlitegan træfe 
 iudith æðele galmoda 
 egesfull afaran eorl nan 
 wiggend aweccan durran 
 gecunnian cumbolwigan 
 halga mægþ habban weorþan 
 metod meowlan mægen nealæhte 
 folc ebrea feohtan þearle 
 heard heoruwæpnum hæfte gieldan 
 hyra fyrngeflitu fagum swyrdum 
 eald æfðoncan assyria wearð 
 dægweorce dom geswiðrod 
 bælc forbiged beorn stodon 
 hyra þeodnes træf þearle gebylde 
 sweorcendferhðe somod 
 onginnan cohhetan cirman hlude 
 gristbitian god orfeorme 
 toðon torn þolian hyra tires 
 eades ellendæda hogedon eorl aweccan 
 hyra winedryhten wiht speow 
 wearð sið læt arod 
 beadorinca burgeteld 
 niðheard neðde he nyd fordraf 
 findan bedd blacne licgan 
 goldgifan gæstes gesne 
 belidenne lungre gefeoll 
 freorig folde onginnan feax teran 
 hreoh mode hrægl somod 
 acwæð wiggendum 
 unrote ute : 
 " wesan geswutelian sylfra forwyrd 
 toweard getacnod tid wesan 
 niðum neah geðrungen sculan nyde losian 
 somod sæcce forweorðan lið sweord geheawen 
 beheafdian healdend " hreowigmode
