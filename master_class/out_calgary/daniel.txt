 hleoðorcyme herian geniman 
 beorht frætwe templ strudon 
 salomanes seld gielpan 
 wearð bliðemod burh aldor 
 gielpan gramlice god anda 
 here hyrran 
 mihtigran mann friðe 
 israela ece 
 tacn wearð starude 
 egeslic eorlum innan heall 
 leodum ligeword cweðan 
 egesan engel drihten 
 let hand hea seld 
 wrat wage word gerynu 
 basu bocstafas burhsittendum 
 wearð folctoga forht mode 
 acul egesan geseon engel hand 
 sele writan sennera wite 
 gyddedon guma mænigeo 
 hæleð heall hand writan 
 beacen burhsittendum 
 werian comon wundor seon 
 sohton sefan gehydum 
 hand writan haliges gastes 
 mihton arædan runcræftige mann 
 engel ærendbec æðelinga cyn 
 daniel drihten gecoren 
 snotor soþfæst seld gangan 
 gaste god cræft 
 georne gefrignan gyfum ceapian 
 burhge weard bocstafas 
 arædan arecan run beodan 
 æcræftig andswarian 
 god spelboda gleaw geðances : 
 " feohsceattum folc beran 
 drihten domas dugeðe cunnan 
 unceapunga orlæg secg 
 word gerynu wendan miht 
 anmedlan æht beran 
 huslfatu halegu hand wer 
 deoflu drincan onginnan 
 israela æ habban 
 god earce gylp beswac 
 windruncen gewitan wurðan sculan 
 aldor willan 
 god goldfatu gylp beran 
 hraðor hremde herian brohte 
 israela gestreon æhte geweald 
 oft cweðan aldor ðeoda 
 soðum word wesan mægen 
 wuldor weard wundor gecyðde 
 gesceafta 
 waldend dom forgifan 
 unscyndne blæd eorþ rices 
 lignest lifgende 
 deoflum dugeþum wealdeð "
